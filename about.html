About Hamdaan Academy

Who We Are

Hamdaan Academy is a trusted educational institution in Tudun Wada, offering both Islamic and Western education in a disciplined and supportive environment.

We are committed to raising students who are academically sound, morally upright, and prepared for success in life.

Our school helps children grow in knowledge, confidence, good manners, and responsibility from Nursery to Senior Secondary level.

Our Vision

To raise students who are academically excellent, morally disciplined, and equipped to contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

To provide quality Islamic and Western education in a safe, structured, and inspiring learning environment.

Our Core Values

Excellence, discipline, responsibility, faith, respect, and continuous growth.
