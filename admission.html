Admission Process

How to Apply

Our admission process is simple and parent-friendly.

01

Make Enquiry

Visit the school or contact us for information about available classes and admission details.

02

Obtain Form

Request and complete the admission form with the correct student information.

03

Submit Documents

Submit the required child documents and relevant details to the school administration.

04

Screening & Approval

The child is screened and placed in the appropriate class level after review.

Classes Available

  • Nursery 1 - 5
  • Primary 1 - 5
  • JSS 1 - 3
  • SS 1 - 3

Required Items

  • Completed admission form
  • Passport photograph
  • Previous school record if available
  • Parent or guardian contact details
